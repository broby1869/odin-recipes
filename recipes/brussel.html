Ingredients

  • 1 pound of brussel sprouts
  • Butter
  • Curry powder
  • Brown sugar
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Heat cast iron skillet
  2. Melt butter
  3. Add brussel sprouts
  4. Add spices and sugar
  5. Mix and then let sit to sear
  6. Mix again and try to sear other side
  7. Let em get crispy
