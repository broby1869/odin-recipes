Ingredients

  • Bone in Chicken Thighs
  • Red Curry Powder
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Rinse and dry chicken thighs
  3. Place in baking dish (I like a baking sheet with parchment paper)
  4. Rub with olive oil
  5. Cover in curry powder, salt, and pepper. Rub them all in well.
  6. Bake for 35-45 minutes, or until internal temp erature reaches 160 degrees
