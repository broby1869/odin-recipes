Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • Pinch of salt
  • Fresh chicken grease

Directions

  1. Boil the water with a pinch of salt
  2. Add rice, cover pot, and reduce to a simmer
  3. Simmer until all the water is absorbed
  4. Do not remove the lid for at least 3 minutes after all water is absorbed
  5. Add chicken grease from the baked chicken thighs
  6. Fluff with fork
